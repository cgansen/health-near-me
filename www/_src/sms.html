Find Health Providers via Text Messaging

You can search healthnear.me with any phone that sends and received text messages. Here's how.

  1. Text your search to 312-548-3951.

    Your message should include a search term, like "services" or "warming center", then the word "near", then an address or location.

    Some samples:

    All services near Wrigley Field
    "services near wrigley field"
    Warming centers near Ashland and Lake Street
    "warming center near ashland at lake st"
    Condom distributors near 2133 South Dearborn Street
    "condoms near 2133 s dearborn st"

  2. Wait a few seconds.

  3. See the results came back, ordered by distance from the location you provided.

SMS Tips

  • Always include as precise an address as possible.
  • Always separate the search term and address with the word "near"; that's how the machines know what to search for and where.
  • Text "list services" to get a list of all services the system knows about.
  • Text "STOP" to block any future messages from healthnear.me, or "START" to resume. We use Twilio to deliver messages; read about the commands they support.
