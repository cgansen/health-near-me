About HealthNear.me

HealthNear.Me is a simple tool for people in Chicago to find public health providers near them.

There are a million ways to use the Internet to find a good restaurant or a place to get your oil changed nearby, but very few to locate much needed health services in your area, such as a mental health or WIC clinic. Existing tools are typically based on a single type of provider, don't work well on mobile devices, and are not searchable by location.

HealthNear.me aggregates nine types of public health providers in one place and makes them searchable, either by this website, which is built to work on smart phones, tables, and old fashioned desktop computers, or via any phone that can send and receive text messages.

This application started as an entry in the Making Public Health Data Work Challenge. The creators, Chris Gansen and Melissa Buenger, are working with the Chicago Department of Public Health to pilot and deploy this tool in 2014.

Data

HealthNear.Me includes data from nine public datasets on the City of Chicago Data Portal:

  • Community Service Centers
  • Condom Distribution Sites
  • Cooling Centers
  • Licensed Substance Abuse Providers
  • Mental Health Clinics
  • Senior Centers
  • STI Specialty Clinic
  • Warming Centers
  • WIC Clinics
For the latest, most accurate information about public health providers, consult the datasets on the Chicago Data Portal. HealthNear.Me contains data accurate as of November 2013.
